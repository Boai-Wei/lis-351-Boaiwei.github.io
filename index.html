Director's Bio

Wong Kar-wai(July 17, 1958 - ) is a Hong Kong contemporary film master and Academy Award Chinese juror. He specializes in the aesthetics of light and shadow, and has won numerous Hong Kong Film Awards and European Film Awards, and was the first Chinese director to win the Palme d'Or at the Cannes Film Festival.
